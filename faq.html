Frequently Asked Questions



What is Flatpay?

Flatpay is a high-quality financial application which allows access to students' financial data, accepts direct bank payments in web or mobile application and student access schools' paying portal without charges or checks, generate receipts accepted by schools to validate payment.

Why use Flatpay?

SEAMLESS TRANSACTION ON THE GO
Get your educational payment across to your school easily without charges.
No DOCUMENTATION DELAY
Authenticated receipt which gives you an edge because FLATPAY receipt carries your school certified authentication.
ALL PAYMENTS, WITH EASE
Not only tuition every other official payments can be done easily with FLATPAY.
BEAT DEADLINES
Receive reminder on your fee payment so you can pay on time and avoid late payment charges.

Is Flatpay available in every country?

No, Flatpay is currently available in only Nigeria.

What are the transaction fees and charges involved?

Flatpay is totally free and allows you to make your payments without extra fees or charges.
